Data Overview 2

Data Sources :

The data for this study comes from airline customer feedback surveys, flight records, and service logs. Additionally, customer complaints and social media reviews help provide insights into dissatisfaction trends. This diverse data allows for a comprehensive analysis of customer experience and service improvements.


System Architecture:

The system architecture for airline customer satisfaction prediction follows a structured pipeline that integrates data collection, processing, machine learning, and visualization to generate actionable insights. After training, the model is deployed on Microsoft Fabric, enabling real-time prediction capabilities for airline businesses.


Data Features

The dataset consists of various features that influence airline customer satisfaction. 

demographic details    : Customer Type, Age

Travel-related factors   : Type of Travel, Class, Flight distance

 Service quality ratings: Seat Comfort, Departure /Arrival time, Food and Drink, Gate Location, Inflight Entertainment, Inflight Wi-Fi, Online Boarding, Legroom Service, Online Boarding, Check-in Service, Baggage Handling.

 Delay information : Departure delay, Arrival Delay
