Creating Clustered column chart (Visual 4) 20
  
    
 To create Dissatisfied customer by Seat Comfort visual in Power BI using the   Clustered column Chart.

Follow these Steps:
  1. From the Visualizations pane, select the 'Clustered Column Chart' and place it in the designated area of your Power BI report.
  2. Assign 'Seat Comfort ' to the X-axis to categorize data. For the column values on the Y-axis, use '%GTSum of Total Customers' 
  3. Add 'Inflight Entertainment' to Legend
  4. Since it is more customer dissatisfied with seat comfort and Inflight Entertainment , I have added satisfaction column on filter field to filter dissatisfied customers .
  5. Name your chart 'Dissatisfied customers distribution by seat comfort and Inflight entertainment' and adjust the visual's title accordingly.                 
  6. Fill up figures provided below for the necessary fields.
          General: Properties, Size,
           Height=220,
           Width=632,
           Position,
           Horizontal=478
            Vertical=493
             Title=Off,
           column
            Apply setting to "High Rating" = #789E11 "green"
                                       "Low Rating" = #D64550 "red"
           Effects, Background =On, Color=#E6E6E6,Transparency=0%, Visual border=off
          Visual: Callout Value, Font=Segeo UI (10),Bold, Font color= #605E5C  (black), Category label=Off
                   Display Units-Auto, Value decimal places-Auto
                   Category Label-Off
