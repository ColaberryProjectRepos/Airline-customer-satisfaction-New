Adding Line and Stacked column Visual (Visual 2) 18

To create a Satisfaction by Class visual in Power BI using the Line and Stacked Column Chart.
    Follow these steps:  
  1. From the Visualizations pane, select the 'Line and Stacked Column Chart' and place it in the designated area of your Power BI report.
  2. Assign 'Class' to the X-axis to categorize data. For the column values on the Y-axis, use 'Total Customers' to represent the total customer rating.
  3. Add line values on the Y-axis for 'Baggage Handling ', 'Seat Comfort ', 'In WIFI ','Inflight Entertainment', 'Legroom service', 'Onboard service',' Online boarding', 'Food and Drink' and 'Gate Location' to illustrate the percentage of satisfaction of each service.
  4. Name your chart 'Satisfaction by Class' and adjust the visual's title accordingly.
  5. For the color scheme, select unique and consistent colors for each service, ensuring they are distinct and avoid confusion with other dashboard elements.
  6. Sync the color coding with other related visuals across the dashboard to maintain a cohesive and intuitive user experience.
