Visualization 4

To better understand customer satisfaction trends, the data was visualized using interactive charts and dashboards in Power BI. These visualizations helped airlines quickly identify areas of improvement. A bar chart was used to show the percentage of satisfied and dissatisfied customers, providing a clear overview of the satisfaction levels. Additionally, segmented visualizations compared customer satisfaction based on factors like travel class, customer type, and age group, allowing airlines to tailor services for different passenger categories. These visual insights help airlines make data-driven decisions and enhance the overall passenger experience.
