Executive Summary 1

Detailed Instructions:
Airline customer satisfaction is a crucial factor in retaining passengers and driving revenue growth. However, over 86% of dissatisfied customers rated inflight entertainment and seat comfort poorly. indicating major areas for improvement. Through data-driven insights, the analysis revealed that improving inflight entertainment, seat comfort, Wi-Fi, and food service could reduce dissatisfaction by over 90%, leading to increased customer retention and improved airline reputation. By implementing targeted improvements, airlines can significantly enhance passenger experience, increase customer loyalty, and boost revenue through strategic service enhancements.


Background :

Airline customer satisfaction plays a vital role in retaining passengers, enhancing airline reputation, and increasing profitability. However, many airlines struggle with dissatisfied passengers due to poor inflight services, including uncomfortable seating, weak Wi-Fi, and low-quality entertainment. To address this challenge, airlines must leverage data analytics and machine learning to identify key dissatisfaction factors and make targeted service improvements. By understanding customer needs, airlines can enhance passenger experience, improve operational efficiency, and drive business growth.



Problem Statement

Airline customer satisfaction is a critical factor in the aviation industry, directly influencing passenger loyalty, brand reputation, and revenue generation. However, many airlines struggle with high dissatisfaction rates, primarily due to factors such as uncomfortable seating, poor inflight entertainment, weak Wi-Fi connectivity, and inadequate food service.



objectives:


The primary objective of this project is to predict airline customer satisfaction using machine learning models and provide actionable insights to enhance the overall passenger experience. Additionally, this project aims to enable future predictions of customer satisfaction trends, ensuring continuous improvement in service offerings and overall customer experience.
