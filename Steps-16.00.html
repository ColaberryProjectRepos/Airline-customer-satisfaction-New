Creating Main KPI and Sub KPI and Indicators 16

1. Select a "Card" visualization and place it in the first box in our Top Left .              This will be the "No of Customers" KPI measure. (The GIF will give you a brief showcase of the formatting you need for this card)
     
         This KPI measure is a No of Customers

         No Of customers = COUNTROWS (Airline_Customer_Satisfaction)
                                     
        Overall Satisfaction = DIVIDE ([Satisfied customers],[No of customers],0)
                                         

     

    For formatting KPI cards in Power BI:

    1.Navigate to Visual > Background and set the color to #CCCCCC
   
    2.For titles, use DIN font at size 20 and set the text color to #118DFF.

    3.Maintain the same format for all KPIs except for the Callout Values color difference.



Adding Sub Kip's and Indicators
