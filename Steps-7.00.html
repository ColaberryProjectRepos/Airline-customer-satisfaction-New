Implementing predictive analytics using machine learning has had a significant impact on understanding and improving airline customer satisfaction. The key impacts of this solution include:


  •  Improved Customer Experience: By identifying key dissatisfaction factors (Seat Comfort, Food & Drink, Inflight Wi-Fi, and Entertainment), airlines can take proactive measures to enhance passenger comfort and service quality.

  • Operational Efficiency & Reduced Complaints: The model helps predict dissatisfaction trends, allowing airlines to address issues before they escalate. Targeted improvements in service areas reduce customer complaints and refund requests, leading to smoother operations.
By leveraging machine learning and data-driven insights, airlines can enhance customer satisfaction, optimize operations, and increase profitability, making it a game-changer for the airline industry.
