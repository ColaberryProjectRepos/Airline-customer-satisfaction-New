Challenges and Solutions:

Used ExtraTreesClassifier for feature importance analysis, identifying that seat comfort, inflight Wi-Fi, and food quality had the most significant impact on customer satisfaction

2.  Running Python code generated by Numtra within Microsoft Fabric proved       challenging, as some code required modifications to ensure compatibility with the Fabric environment.

3.  Created visual reports and case studies to help airlines understand and act   on insights, such as improving inflight entertainment, enhancing meal options, and reducing flight delays to boost satisfaction and revenue.


Lessons Learned:

  • Importance of Data Preparation : Ensuring clean, complete, and structured data significantly improves model accuracy and reliable insights. Handling missing values, outliers and feature selection plays a key role in predictive analytics.
  • Customer Satisfaction is Influenced by Multiple Factors : Service areas like seat comfort, inflight Wi-Fi, food & drink, and baggage handling have the biggest impact on passenger experience. Operational delays significantly reduce satisfaction, requiring proactive solutions to improve punctuality.
  • Data Visualization Enhances Decision-Making: Power BI dashboards provided real-time KPIs, helping airline executives make data-driven service enhancements.Visual reports made it easier to communicate customer trends, operational gaps, and improvement strategies. 
  • Predictive Analytics Can Drive Revenue Growth: By personalizing services based on customer preferences, airlines can increase customer retention and upsell premium services (Wi-Fi upgrades, better seating, meal plans).Business strategies based on data insights help reduce operational costs and increase profitability


Future Capabilities:

The future capabilities of the Airline Customer Satisfaction Prediction Project focus on real-time predictive analytics, AI-powered engagement, and enhanced personalization. Airlines can leverage real-time dashboards to track passenger sentiment and take immediate action on service issues. AI chatbots and sentiment analysis will enable personalized customer interactions and proactive complaint resolution by analyzing social media reviews and surveys. Automated flight delay predictions and real-time IoT integrations will enhance the passenger experience by monitoring in-flight comfort and providing proactive service adjustments. Additionally, hyper-personalized marketing and loyalty programs will help airlines increase customer retention and revenue by offering tailored rewards and exclusive discounts. These advancements will ensure seamless travel experiences, improve operational efficiency, and drive long-term business growth in the airline industry.
